Smart Daily Blogs

Ten niche blogs, all written by Kkuma Park — an indie writer in Seoul. Honest, first-person notes on finance, health, tech, cooking, home repair, parenting, pets, travel, and cars.

Hi, I'm Kkuma Park. I spent more than a decade in IT, got burned out, and started rebuilding my life one topic at a time — money, health, my old apartment, my dog, the cars I bought (and regretted), the meals I actually cook after 10pm. Along the way I kept notes.

These ten blogs are those notes, cleaned up and published. Every article is written from my own experience and my own data. I use AI tools to help draft outlines, but I review, edit, and fact-check every piece before it goes live. See the About page for the full story and how I run these sites.
